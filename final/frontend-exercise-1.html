Exercise Instructions

Create a reusuable slideshow widget using the the images below.

Requirements

  1. The transition time between slides should be configurable, but defaults to 5 seconds.
  2. Show only slide at a time.
  3. Bonus points for readability, originality, and extendibility.

Feel free to use a JavaScript library (e.g., jQuery, Prototype, YUI, ExtJs, dojo) to support your work but please do not use any plugins or pre-built widgets. There are plenty of implementations and examples out there. However, you cannot simply copy those examples or an already created widget.

Creating this widget should demonstrate your knowledge of JavaScript, and show off your software design skills. Include implementation notes. No fancy transition effects required.
